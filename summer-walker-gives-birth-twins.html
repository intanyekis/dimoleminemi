Summer Walker Gives Birth To Twins: Im So Proud Of Myself

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Summer Walker has given birth to her newest bundle of joy – surprising the world at the same time when she revealed she was actually carrying twins.

In a since-deleted post to her Instagram page on Sunday (January 1), the singer revealed she’d welcomed two new members of her family, adding how proud of herself she was for doing so.

AD LOADING...

“I’m so proud of myself,” Summer wrote. “Just sharing to inspire other women, cause I know once you carry twins to almost 42 weeks, especially with one breech, people will try to steer you towards induction or C-section. You can do it, this was my second home birth all natural 7 hrs, no tearing & I couldn’t of done it without my spirit guides, Godparents, birth team, my elders, & the best dad doula ever Larry lol.”

“He was so hands on the whole time I was really impressed it wasn’t easy but it gets done,” she continued. “Both births I almost blacked out at the end but eating your placenta will definitely keep you above water I have thin blood so I always end up going to the hospital to bring myself back into good strength for them but as long as my kids stay at home untouched I’m good.”

Follow your instincts. – mama badoula”
