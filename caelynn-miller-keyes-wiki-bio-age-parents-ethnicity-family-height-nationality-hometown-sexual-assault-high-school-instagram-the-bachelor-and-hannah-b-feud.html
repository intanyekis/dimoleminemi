Caelynn Miller-Keyes Biography – Caelynn Miller-Keyes Wiki

Caelynn Miller-Keyes is Miss North Carolina 2018, first runner-up in the Miss USA pageant in May 2018 and a contestant on season 23 of The Bachelor.

She is from Asheville, North Carolina and works as a social media consultant in Asheville, North Carolina. As a small child, she was given a 10% chance to walk again after struggling with a life threatening illness.

Caelynn attended Virginia Commonwealth University from where she graduated with a degree in broadcast journalism.

Caelynn Miller-Keyes Sexual Assault

She is a survivor of sexual assault. In her “Meet Miss North Carolina” video, Caelynn revealed she was sexually assaulted during her sophomore year at Virginia Commonwealth University at a fraternity house by two men she had known for a long time.

She is a dedicated advocate for sexual assault prevention by speaking to high school and college students about the importance of consent. She has been instrumental in helping victims have voices by changing the Title IX policies to protect college students, having survived more than one attack personally.

Caelynn Miller-Keyes and Hannah Brown Feud

Caelynn and Hannah competed together for the Miss USA title in 2018. They were roommates at Miss USA. According to Caelynn, Hannah “kind of snapped” when she was named First Runner-up and they had a huge falling-out.

“We’re not necessarily the best of friends,” Caelynn said. “We were roommates at Miss USA. We were super close, and then she wasn’t happy that I was first runner-up. She was mad that she didn’t place, for sure. There were a lot of hurt feelings.”

Hannah apparently has a beef with Caelyn, too. She called her ex-rommie “fake.”

“It’s just so funny because there’s not one person in the world that I have a problem with, except her,” Hannah said. “It totally scares me for Colton.”
