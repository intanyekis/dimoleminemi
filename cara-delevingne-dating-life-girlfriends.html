Cara Delevingne's Dating Life Is As Messy As It Is Fascinating

July 2024 · 7 minute read

Ever since model Cara Delevingne rose to fame in the early 2010s, her love life has been in the spotlight. The 30-year-old is known for being free-spirited and adventurous and that surely shows in her relationships.Related: Cara Delevingne's New Home Is Worth Double Her Last And We Understand WhyToday, we're taking a closer look at the model's love life. From her serious relationship to short hookups — keep on scrolling to see which stars Cara Delevingne has been with over the years!

12 Cara Delevingne Identifies As A Pansexual

Cara Delevingne has been open about identifying as a pansexual meaning that she’s attracted to all genders. "I always will remain, I think, pansexual," Delevingne said to Variety. "However one defines themselves, whether it’s ‘they’ or ‘he’ or ‘she,’ I fall in love with the person — and that’s that. I’m attracted to the person."

11 Why Did Cara Delevingne And Ashley Benson Break Up After Two Years?

Cara Delevingne and Pretty Little Liars star Ashley Benson were first linked to each other in April 2018, but the two didn't go Instagram official until the 50th anniversary of the Stonewall Riots. "I don't know because it is Pride, it's been 50 years since Stonewall happened and I don't know," the model told E! News. "It's been just about our one-year anniversary so, why not?"

Even though the two seemed to have a strong relationship, the COVID-19 pandemic put them through a real test which they didn't survive. "It was the most trying time," Delevingne told Cosmpolitan, adding that all that time being at home together was difficult for the two. "But it wasn’t just the relationship that was more difficult to manage in lockdown; it was the breakup, too. [It] made me deal with it more, which was harder. Or better. I don’t know. Everything is magnified in a pandemic."

10 The Truth About Cara Delevingne's Threesome With Amber Heard And Elon Musk

In 2016, Cara Delevingne was rumored to have a three-way affair with Elon Musk and Amber Heard (who was at the time still married to Hollywood star Johnny Depp). In a testimony given by Joshua Drew (Amber Heard's friend) in November 2019, he revealed that Heard told him she spent a night with the model and the Tesla CEO.

9 Did Rita Ora Write A Song About Her Relationship With Cara Delevingne?

Fans believe that Rita Ora‘s 2018 song "Girls" was about her relationship with Cara Delevingne. The two have at the time been close friends for a while, and a source for Page Six revealed that the model was the inspiration behind the song. "Rita and Cara’s relationship has always been speculated about and questions were asked at the time about whether they were more than good friends. While they never confirmed or denied anything, Rita has definitely allowed her music to do the talking," the source revealed. "She has been honest with pals about what went on with her and Cara and, during the recording process in the studio, she was happy to discuss the model being the main influence behind the track."

8 Cara Delevingne And Margaret Qualley Shared A Mask Kiss

In July 2020 the model was spotted giving a masked kiss to actress Margaret Qualley after the two grabbed lunch together. Because of this, some assumed that the two might be more than just friends but the truth is that Cara Delevingne just seems to be very affectionate with her friends.

7 What Happened Between Cara Delvingne And Halsey?

Cara Delvingne has been linked to musician Halsey after starring in her music video for the song "Nightmare." Both, Delevingne and Halsey have just gotten out of relationships (the model and actress Ashley Benson ended things, and so did Halsey and actor Evan Peters), and it seemed as if they just wanted to have fun together.

6 Did Cara Delevingne Date Michel Jackson's Daughter?

Cara Delevingne has a close friendship with Michael Jackson's daughter Paris, and the two ladies even got matching tattoos in April 2021. Because Delevingne and Jackson have been seen together at parties, fans began wondering whether the two ever dated. "They have a flirty friendship but aren't dating or in a relationship," a source told People in 2018. "Paris is 19 years old and living her life. She has no plans to settle down any time soon."

5 Cara Delevingne Reportedly Hooked Up With Miley Cyrus

Miley Cyrus and Cara Delevingne became friends in 2013, and over the years they were linked to each other numerous times. "[Miley’s] been getting close to Cara lately. They’ve known each other for a while but they’re starting to get super tight," a source told Hollywood Life in 2013. "They have fun together. Miley thinks she's super cool. She's a big fan of Cara right now." The two ladies have shared photos of their kisses, but as previously mentioned — Cara Delevingne is very affectionate with her friends.

4 Long Distance Caused Cara Delevingne And St. Vincent's Breakup

After a year and a half together, Cara Delevingne and St. Vincent ended things because they couldn't handle the constant distance. Considering that Cara Delevingne is a model and St. Vincent is a musician, both ladies were frequently on the road because of fashion events and concerts. However, it seems as if they ended things on good terms, and St. Vincent has even admitted that dating the model was a big part of her life.

3 Did Cara Delevingne Date Harry Styles?

Despite the fact that numerous outlets reported back in 2013 that Cara Delevingne is dating the at-the-time One Direction member, the two were most likely never anything more than just good friends. During an appearance on Chelsea, Cara Delevingne even joked that the two look related. "Yeah, we look at each other, and we’re like… is this a mirror?," Delevingne said. "Yeah, we definitely have some similar things going on."

2 Cara Delevingne's Whirlwind Relationship With Michelle Rodriguez

Even though Cara Delevingne and actress Michelle Rodriguez dated for only four months in 2014, the two stars didn't hide their relationship. They were frequently spotted running errands and attending sports events, however, it seems as if their 14-year age gap and different lifestyles ended up causing their breakup. "Michelle got tired of Cara constantly being pictured with other female celebs," a source told The Sun. "Michelle isn't as interested in being in the limelight. She thinks they have different priorities."

1 Did Cara Delevingne Hook Up With Jaden Smith?

On Valentine's Day 2021, Cara Delevingne was spotted hugging and kissing actor and musician Jaden Smith whom she stars alongside in the 2020 movie Life in a Year. After the movie premiered, the model gushed about Smith to Entertainment Weekly saying "I love Jaden so much, I adore him with all my heart. He is a phenomenal artist but I’m so glad that people are going to be able to see him as an actor like this. And us having to shave our heads at the same time, he’ll be in my life forever." While the fact that they spent Valentine's Day together surely meant something, their relationship (if there was one) didn't last long.
