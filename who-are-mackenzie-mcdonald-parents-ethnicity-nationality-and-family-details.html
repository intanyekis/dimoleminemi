Who Are Mackenzie Mcdonald Parents?

Mackenzie Mcdonald was born to parents Vivian Young and Michael Mcdonald.

Michael’s father is an oral surgeon, whereas his mother works as an artist and photographer. They also have a daughter named Dana, who is a gymnast and a graduate of UCLA.

The tennis player was introduced to the sport by his dad at the early age of 3. Both of his parents come from California and are still residing there till the present day.

Apart from his job as a surgeon, Michael is very interested in sports and plays tennis during his free time. Because of his keen interest in sports, both his children are athletic and have been involved in the sport they are quite fond of.

Talking about his mother, Vivian, she is a creative person who creates different kinds of art that she has learned while traveling to different places.

She seems like a fun and creative person who often searches for inspiration for her next new creation. The artist has her own studio named Vivianesque, which is located in Alameda, California.

Mackenzie Mcdonald Ethnicity And Nationality

Mackenzie Mcdonald belongs to a mixed ethnicity since her parents have Chinese, Scottish, and English backgrounds.

His father is of Scottish and English Heritage, whereas his mother is of Chinese descent. Both of them might have been raised in California, so their nationality is American.

Mackenzie and his sister have been raised in a multicultural environment and often celebrate the various festivals linked with their culture.
