Mark Anthony Aguirre’s Wiki Biography

Mark Anthony Aguirre, born on 10 December 1959 in Chicago, Illinois USA, is a retired basketball player of Mexican descent. He is best known for his stints with the Dallas Mavericks and Detroit Pistons in the National Basketball Association (NBA), winning two titles with the latter team.

Sources state that Aguirre has a fortune of over $3 million, as of early 2017, earned mainly through his involvement in basketball.

Mark Aguirre’s Net Worth: $3 Million

Aguirre attended George Westinghouse High School in Chicago and later played for Chicago’s DePaul University’s Blue Demons basketball team. He left college after his junior year and became the first overall pick by the Dallas Mavericks in the 1981 NBA draft.

During his time with the team, Aguirre registered notable scoring stats which earned him popularity and a significant net worth. His career with the Mavericks, though filled with high-scoring efforts, was also marked by strained relationships and frequent conflicts with coach Dick Motta and several players.

He was then traded to the Detroit Pistons in the 1988–89 season, and played a crucial part in the team’s back-to-back NBA titles. His popularity and wealth grew during his four seasons with the Pistons, despite being limited due to injuries and player dynamics.

After retiring from professional basketball in 1994, Aguirre remained involved in the sport. He served in various coaching and development roles for teams like the Mavericks, Indiana Pacers, and New York Knicks. These endeavors further contributed to his financial success.

In his personal life, Aguirre has been married to Angela Bowman since 1988, and the couple has four children together.
