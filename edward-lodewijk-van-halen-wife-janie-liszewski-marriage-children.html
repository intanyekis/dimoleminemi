Edward Lodewijk Van Halen was an American-Dutch musician. He had been married twice in his life. Read more to find about his married life.

Eddie Van Halen’s death

Eddie Van Halen died on 6th October 2020 due to cancer. The singer was suffering from throat cancer and was undergoing the treatment since quiet sometime. Eddie was survived by his wife and son. His son paid the tribute on social media with the caption,

“I can’t believe I’m having to write this, but my father, Edward Lodewijk Van Halen, has lost his long and arduous battle with cancer this morning.

He was the best father I could ever ask for. Every moment I’ve shared with him on and off stage was a gift.

My heart is broken and I don’t think I’ll ever fully recover from this loss.

I love you so much, Pop.”

Is Edward still married to Janie Liszewski?

Edward Lodewijk Van Halen dated Janie Liszewski for three years before walking down the aisle. Edward Lodewijk walked down the aisle on 27th June 2009 with Janie Liszewski. They married at his Studio City home.

Also, Janie was his publicist and she wore a  champagne-colored v-neck wedding dress by Hanna Hartnell. Eddie was wearing white pants by Sy Devore and Max Nason flip flops. Similarly, they said their vows under an altar of white roses constructed by Mark’s Garden. Around 100 guests at their wedding.

Eddie’s son served as his best man and his brother was an ordained minister. They had planned their honeymoon in New York and the Netherlands. They are still together even after more than 10 years of marriage. Likewise, they are living together happily.

Edward Lodewijk Van Halen ex-wife and children

Edward was married to actress Valerie Bertinelli in the past. They met each other on 29th August 1980 at a Van Halen concert in Shreveport, Louisiana. On 11th April 1981, they walked down the aisle. Together they have a son.

Their son Wolfgang was born on 16th March 1991. He is an American musician who is currently the bassist for his father Van Halen. Similarly, he is also the former bassist for the heavy metal band Tremonti.

Likewise, he recorded his first album with his father in 2011 A Different Kind of Truth. Also, his mother Valerie is an American actress and television personality who played Barbara Cooper Royer in One Day at a Time.

She is known for her work in Apple’s Way, One Day at a Time, Battle of the Network Stars III, The Hardy Boys, The Promise of Love, Fridays, PM Magazine, and Food Network Star.

Similarly, the couple separated in 2001, and on 5th December 2005, she filed for divorce. She claimed that the reason for their divorce was Van’s cocaine addiction and his refusal to quit smoking despite being diagnosed with oral cancer and losing one-third of his tongue in the treatment process. Their divorce was finalized in 2007 December.
