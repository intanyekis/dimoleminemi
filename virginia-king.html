Virginia Guynes married Dan Guynes three months after welcoming Demi. Morgan Guynes was born to the couple. Meanwhile, Charles, her first husband, had another child.

Dan, her second husband, played a significant role in Demi’s upbringing. The family traveled extensively as a result of Dan’s work. Their marriage suffered as a result of the traveling and poverty, which was exacerbated by the fact that both were heavy drinkers.
