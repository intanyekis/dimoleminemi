Overall Meaning

The 1975's song Happiness is a captivating track that explores the idea of happiness and love. The song begins with the concept of confidence being comical, suggesting that sometimes, self-assuredness can come across as arrogant or even amusing. However, the singer is happy, and he clarifies that happiness, for him, stems from doing something he knows is good, indicating the importance of respecting oneself and one's actions.


The chorus introduces a woman who has taught the singer what love is. He describes her as "insatiable," likening her body to modern art, further emphasizing her uniqueness and beauty. However, he also feels that he is messing up the relationship, and despite calling out her name, he fears that he may never love again. The final part of the song repeats the idea of "show me your love," suggesting that vulnerability and openness are necessary for genuine happiness and love to flourish.


Overall, the song explores the complexity of human emotions, emphasizing the importance of self-love and vulnerability.
