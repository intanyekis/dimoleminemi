Anirudh Ravichander Facts

  • He made his debut in the Tamil industry at the age of 21.
  • His debut song Why This Kolaveri Di which was made for the 2012 film 3, went frenzy across the globe and has amassed more than 200 million views on YouTube.
  • He’s one of the most popular composers in South India.
  • Besides Tamil Cinema, he has also worked in Telugu and Hindi.
  • As of December 2019, he has won 9 South Indian International Movie Awards.
  • He has signed a record deal with Sony Music.
  • During the Diwali 2016, along with international artist Diplo, he released the remix of Major Lazer and MØ‘s hit Cold Water.
  • Ravichander won the Chennai Times’ “Most Desirable Man 2017″ poll and was hailed as the first musician to win the poll.
  • He was part of a band in school called Zinx.
  • At the age of 10, he started composing music and landed his break at the age of 22.
  • He was a School Pupil Leader at Padma Seshadri Bala Bhavan, K.K Nagar.
  • He loves music and composing is his passion.
  • His dream was to pursue a career in music and become a musician. Back in high school, he participated in a TV show judged by A. R. Rahman and his band was among the winners along with the other 5 bands. He was awarded the “Best Instrumentalist” by his idol.
  • As a music composer, he made his debut in his cousin Aishwarya R. Dhanush‘s directorial debut in 3 starring Dhanush.
  • On Valentine’s eve in 2016, he released a single titled Avalukena under the banner of Sony Music.
  • He sang and composed a single for the Indian Tamil-language drama film David (2013).
  • His soundtrack Vanakkam Chennai which had been released in July 2013 was a chart-topping one that reached the top spot in iTunes “India Top Album” category shortly after its release. Prior to the release, Ravichander revealed that it was his “best work”.
  • His promotional song Chennai City Gangster had placed him in a collaboration with Adhi of Hiphop Tamizha and British-Indian rapper Hard Kaur.
  • As of December 2019, Anirudh has compiled more than 40 awards for his works.
  • Anirudh reportedly charged ₹10 crore for Shah Rukh Khan-starrer Jawan (2023).
