Dalila Bela is a Canadian actress who is the eldest of 3 siblings born in Montreal, Quebec in 2001. At the age of 5, she started her acting career with her 1st national commercial. Her family moved to Vancouver, British Columbia when she was 6 years old and she has gone on with her acting career. Both her younger brothers Bruce Salomon and Raphael Alejandro, are actors.

She has acted in television and movies and is known for her performances in the series Odd Squad where she plays the role of Agent Olive, a young government agent who uses math to investigate changes in circumstances in her town. She has acted in the Netflix series, Anne as Diana Berry, and performed the role of Sydney in the animation Ready Jet Go!

Dalila Bela had won 4 awards and received 13 nominations even before she turned 20. She won Young Artist Award for “Best Performance” in The Stranger, and Joanna Makes a Friend. She won the 2015 Joey Award for her part in Odd Squad as well.
