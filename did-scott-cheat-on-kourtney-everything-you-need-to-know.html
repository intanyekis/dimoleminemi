Did Scott Cheat On Kourtney? Everything You Need To Know!

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Scott Disick and Kourtney Kardashian have had a difficult relationship throughout the years. The couple had been together for about a decade, and there had been accusations that Disick had been disloyal throughout that time. Their relationship ended after he again mistreated Kardashian, but did he ever really cheat on her?

The relationship between Disick and Kardashian was full of drama.

In 2006, they met at a house party where they both had friends. By 2007, they were dating, and no one would have thought that they would be together for almost a decade.

At first, Kardashian’s family didn’t really like Disick. They thought he was rude and needed to stop drinking so much.

Disick’s behavior caused them to fight a lot, and he drank too much — something he has admitted in the past — right up until they broke up for good in 2015.

In 2008, his sisters discovered text messages on his phone

Disick and Kardashian’s honeymoon period ended around the time of the premiere of Keeping Up With the Kardashians. In one episode, Kardashian sisters Khloé and Kim Kardashian looked through Disick’s phone and discovered that he had texted another lady.

Disick expressed his desire to spend a week with the woman “without telling Kourt,” according to the messages. Even though it was tough to convey such bad news, Khloé and Kim told their sister what they had discovered.

Kardashian then claimed Disick of cheating on her with his ex-girlfriend, and the two ended their relationship. She and her sister Khloé were filming Kourtney and Khloé Take Miami around the same time.

After the couple formally divorced, they had to work together to co-parent their three children. They went to counseling together (and still do), and it helped them grow as friends.

For more than two years, Disick has been dating Sofia Richie, and Kardashian and Richie get along swimmingly. Disick and Richie have even gone on family holidays with the Kardashians, and Disick once shared a photo of himself sitting between the two women in a lounge chair.
