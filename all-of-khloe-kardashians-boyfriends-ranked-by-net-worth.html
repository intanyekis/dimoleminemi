All Of Khloe Kardashian's Boyfriends, Ranked By Net Worth

After her father, Robert Kardashian became a part of the defense team for OJ Simpson; the Kardashian family first received media attention. In 2007, the family was offered a reality series Keeping Up With The Kardashians, on E Network to showcase their professional and personal exploits to audiences. The show became an instant hit and made the Kardashian-Jenner clan A-list stars. Besides the main series, Khloe starred in two spin-off series: Kourtney & Khloe Take the Hamptons and Khloé & Lamar. She also co-owned the DASH Clothing store with her sisters and has released several books. More recently, Kardashian launched a new clothing line Good American to sell comfortable apparel.

Each Kardashian-Jenner sister has been in the public eye from a young age as all eyes are glued to the family’s work and romantic relationships. Khloe Kardashian first stepped into the dating scene in 2009 and has had several high-profile relationships. Let’s look at all of Khloe Kardashian’s boyfriends, ranked by their net worth.

Trey Songz ($12 million)

After a brief relationship with James Harden, it was speculated that Khloe Kardashian was dating Odell Beckham Jr., but it turned out that she had a fling with rapper Trey Songz. According to US Weekly, the couple dated for a few months in 2016 and were first spotted celebrating Khloe’s 32nd birthday at Dave & Busters. Known for his modern hip-hop music, Songz is a prolific Grammy-nominated rapper who has sold more than 25 million albums around the world. He has collaborated with several artists, including Gucci Mane, Nicki Minaj, and Drake.

Rick Fox ($20 million)

Rick Fox has had a prolific NBA career during his time, made over $34 million in salary, and most notably played for the Boston Celtics and Los Angeles Lakers. He first played in the NBA in 1991 and retired by the end of the 2003-04 season. He owns an eSports team and has appeared in television shows and movies. Fox was publicly spotted on a dinner date with Khloe Kardashian in May 2015, where they looked cozy together in Casa Vega.

French Montana ($22 million)

Another successful rapper on the list, French Montana, is a rapper and a record producer who co-founded the Coke Boys Production. Montana has made over 2.1 million sales throughout his career, and his highest-grossing album was his sophomore hit, Jungle Rules. It landed on the No.3 spot on Billboard 200 and sold 1.1 million copies. Montana and Khloe dated in 2014 for a little less than a year, as noted by Page Six. Kardashian mentioned that she felt rushed into the relationship and decided to end things.

Lamar Odom ($30 million)

In one of her most highly-publicized relationships, Khloe Kardashian married Lamar Odom in September 2009 after a month of dating. The couple filed for divorce after four years of marriage, but Khloe put the divorce on hold after Odom overdosed at a Nevada brothel in 2015. After taking care of him, they divorced in 2016. In 1999, Odom was picked during an NBA Draft by the Los Angeles Clippers and performed well during his rookie season. The NBA star made $115 million in salary during his career.

Tristan Thompson ($45 million)

Khloe Kardashian and Tristan Thompson have been together since 2016 in an on-again-off-again relationship. As mentioned by People, they were first spotted together at a Drake concert in 2016 and appeared on Keeping Up With The Kardashians in 2017. The couple now has two children together and has endured various ups and downs. A Canadian-born player, Tristan Thompson, has been playing with Cleveland Cavaliers since being the No.4 NBA draft in 2011.

Matt Kemp ($75 million)

Professional baseball player Matt Kemp first made his debut in 2007, at just 22 years of age. After playing with several teams, he signed up with Los Angeles Dodgers in 2018 and earned $150 million in salary. He also is a philanthropist as the MLB star involves himself in various charities and sponsors children to watch his games. During the peak of his budding career, Kemp dated Khloe Kardashian in 2013 when she had recently split from Lamar Odom. They were together at concerts for Drake, Jay-Z, and John Legend.

James Harden ($165 million)

James Harden has been long considered one of the best players in the NBA and has become an eight-time NBA All-Star. Harden is a skilled shooting guard who also won a gold medal at the 2012 London Olympics. With an annual salary of $44 million and additional endorsement deals, he is the wealthiest boyfriend of Khloe Kardashian. The pair met in the summer of 2015 during Kanye West’s birthday party and began dating, as stated by The Sun. They dated for not more than nine months before ending things.

Khloe Kardashian has also had several flings during the early years of her fame with notable stars, including Rashad McCants and Derrick Ward. The youngest Kardashian underwent surrogacy treatment with Tristan Thompson for a year and had another child with him, a son, on September 22, 2022.
