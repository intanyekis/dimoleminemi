Mae Whitman Biography & Profile Summary

Born on June 9, 1988, in Los Angeles, California, Mae Whitman hails from a family immersed in the entertainment industry. Even after her parents’ divorce when she was just three, Mae’s dedication to her career remained steadfast from her early school days.

At the tender age of two, Mae showcased her talent for voice acting in a commercial. By six, she secured a role in “When A Man Loves A Woman,” competing with over 700 girls during auditions. This marked the start of her journey in the world of acting.

Venturing into Music

Aside from acting, Mae delved into the music industry, recording singles and lending her vocals to TV series and film soundtracks. She also appeared in music videos, such as “I Was An Idiot” and “Sweetheart.”

Mae Whitman Awards and Recognitions
