How Is Justin Bieber's Relationship With Hailey's Dad, Stephen Baldwin?

Anyone who has followed Hailey Bieber over the years knows she has a reputation for being religious. Justin Bieber, on the other hand, not so much. Justin's wife just shared an interview she recorded with actress Yvonne Orji on her YouTube page. The two stars sat down for a talk about attempting to negotiate, hanging on to their religious convictions while working in the entertainment world. Hailey revealed that she often sees comments on social media that she believes may be highly judgemental and can come from fans with the same Christian background.

The model added, "I felt weird about posting certain photos of myself or feeling like, 'People in the church are gonna see this. Am I doing something wrong? Am I setting a bad example?'"

While defending herself from critics, Baldwin also revealed that their faith is the most significant part of her relationship with Bieber. Hailey said: "They ask me all the time, 'What would you say is the biggest thing in your relationship? You guys are really happy.' And I'm like, 'It's our faith.' It's what we believe in. If we didn't have that, we wouldn't even be here. We wouldn't even be together."

As religion is vital in Hailey's family, Stephen Baldwin appreciates Justin's respect for their traditions. A source told People, "Stephen appreciated the respect that Justin has shown him as Hailey's father. It sounds old-fashioned, but it's a sign of respect. Justin and Hailey are very traditional when it comes to those things, and Stephen, of course, is, as well."

Has Justin Bieber Made Peace With His Past?

Hailey's live interview with Hillsong Church's Youtube Channel showed why Bieber may have negatively influenced his wife; it wasn't until the two talked to Vogue that even more interesting details came up. Following this, he also discussed his drug issues and that the couple almost fell out because of it. Considering Hailey's overly religious past, it becomes apparent that Justin's experiences with drugs and sex may not have positively impacted his wife.

"I found myself doing things that I was so ashamed of, being super-promiscuous and stuff, and I think I used Xanax because I was so ashamed," he said as he recalled those memories during the interview.

Although things are better now for Justin Bieber regarding his drug usage, he almost hinted that his wife had to put in all the effort to bring the two to where they are now. Meanwhile, Hailey's dad seems to adore Justin and respect their relationship.
